What We Do

AnalyzeThisContract is an AI-powered contract analysis platform. You upload or paste any lease agreement or contract, and our system analyzes it using advanced AI to provide a clear, structured breakdown in plain English.

We identify potential risks, flag unusual or missing clauses, extract key terms, and provide actionable negotiation tips — all in seconds. No legal jargon. No waiting for a lawyer. No surprises on move-in day.

The service is delivered entirely online via our web application. No software download is required. Analyses are performed instantly upon submission and are not legal advice.

Supported Document Types

AnalyzeThisContract is optimized for residential and commercial lease agreements, rental contracts, sublease agreements, and similar legal documents. You can submit your contract in two ways:

PDF upload — Upload any PDF up to 10MB directly from your device. Our system extracts the text automatically.

Paste text — Copy and paste the full text of your contract into the input field for immediate analysis.

We do not currently support image-only scanned PDFs (documents where the text is embedded as an image rather than selectable text).
